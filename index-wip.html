Events

We hold meetups monthly and workshops regularly.

Our meetups let you:

  • Share things you're excited about with other makers;
  • See what other people are up to and contribute;
  • Find people to work together with on interesting projects;
  • Interested in giving a talk on something you love? Contact us!

Our workshops:

  • Reach out to the public to get more people interested in technology.
  • Cover all sorts of topics: robots, Christmas trees, retro games, 3d-printed Pokemon, machine learning...
  • Interested in running a workshop? Hit us up!
